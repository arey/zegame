Development Team

Here is a brief presentation of each of the 3 french members of ZeGame.

Antoine Rey 

He's a 3rd year student in the computer science department of the National Institute of Applied Science in Lyon. He is mad about computer programming and more particulary in video game development. A few years ago he alredy has done a set of little games and demos. Since 2 years now he's developing ZeGame in its spare time.

Principal coder, he has realized not only the main motor of the game, but also all the necessary tools that allow to create the level maps and to edit animations of the characters. He uses C++ Builder from Inprise and Visual C++ 6 from Microsoft as coding tools. More he also uses Pro Motion 4 and Sound Forge 4.5 to work with digital sounds and pictures.

Don't hesitate to contact him at the following adress : antoine.rey@insa-lyon.fr

 

Joël Mouclier

Illustrator of comics : Semio or Dragons (at Delcourt editions), outstanding and talented infographist, he works at the present time for Infogrames since already one year, in their Techlab laboratory.

All the graphims you can see in the game are from him : animations, textures, logos, ... It made it on software as 3D studio Max, Photoshop or the less knowm Pro Motion, the worthy successor of Deluxe Paint Animation. More over, throught its art work, he created the atmosphere of the game and this surrealist universe.
