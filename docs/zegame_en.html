 Welcome in the world of Nains, a fairy and bewitching world where live maleficent creatures, erudite dwarfs, alchemist monks, harebrained ogres and ... peaceful humans (of course ;-). You will incarnate a young hero we will evolve in this universe to your liking.

 
    ZeGame is a single 3D Isometric role playing game that combines the best of the features of Zelda and Ultima series. The whole in an epic fantasy virtual world. The sotry takes place a long time ago in a medieval world where some inhabitant have the secret of strange kind of steam engines of our late XIX century. These web site is dedicated as well to the development and the implementation of this free CRPG as well to rpg player that are looking for a new gaming experience, so at all the fans of Baldur's Gate, Zelda, Ultima Underworld, Dagerfall, Final, Leged of Mana ...
